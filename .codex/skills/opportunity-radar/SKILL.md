# opportunity-radar

## Purpose

Convert broad opportunity hunting into structured watchlists and executable research tasks.

## Output

- Opportunity name.
- Why it matters.
- Required data.
- Fast test path.
- Expected cost.
- Risk.
- Next action.

## Rules

- Favor low-cost tests.
- Separate evidence from guesses.
- Produce ranked shortlists, not long vague lists.
