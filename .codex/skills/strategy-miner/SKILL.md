# strategy-miner

## Purpose

Mine strategy ideas and turn them into structured, testable research tasks.

## Input types

- Public strategy notes.
- Factor ideas.
- Market observations.
- User hypotheses.
- Forum or article summaries.
- Existing project reports.

## Output structure

For each candidate idea, write:

1. Idea name.
2. Core logic.
3. Data required.
4. Entry condition.
5. Exit condition.
6. Risk controls.
7. Fast simulation path.
8. Why it may fail.
9. Next repository task.

## Rules

- Keep all work simulation-only unless separately authorized.
- Separate evidence from speculation.
- Prefer small tests before large builds.
- Do not claim profitability without tests.
- Convert vague ideas into explicit rules.
- Put output in `REPORTS/strategy_miner_report.md` or a task-specific report.
